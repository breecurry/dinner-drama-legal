🍳 Dinner Drama

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This is the privacy policy for Dinner Drama, the sassy AI chef app that helps you decide what's for dinner. It's written in plain English because privacy policies should actually be readable.

Who we are

Dinner Drama ("we," "us," or "our") operates this app. If you have questions about this policy or your data, reach us at support@duraventi.com.

1. What Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and a hashed (encrypted) version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.

Content you create

When you use Dinner Drama, we collect the content you generate:

  • Messages you send to the AI chef (your chat history)
  • Ingredients you type into the pantry feature
  • Photos you take of your fridge or pantry

This content is used to generate meal suggestions and recipe recommendations for you.

Usage data

We collect information about how you use the app — which features you use, meals you save or rate, and actions like "I Cooked This" — to personalise your experience and improve suggestions over time.

Push notification tokens

If you allow push notifications, we store a device token so we can send you reminders and updates. You can turn off notifications at any time in your device settings.

In-app purchase records

When you purchase credit packs, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive a receipt confirmation from Apple but do not handle or store your payment card details. Apple's privacy policy governs the payment process.

Referral data

If you participate in the referral program, we store your referral code and track how many people have used it, so we can credit your account.

Analytics and tracking

Dinner Drama does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and provide the Dinner Drama service
  • Generate personalised meal suggestions, recipes, and grocery lists
  • Remember your preferences, saved meals, and cooking history
  • Send you push notifications (with your permission)
  • Process and credit in-app purchases and referrals
  • Improve the app and fix problems

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

3. Third-Party AI Providers

This is important: the core features of Dinner Drama — including meal suggestions, recipe generation, fridge photo analysis, grocery lists, and AI chef chat — are powered by third-party AI services.

When you use these features, the content you submit (your chat messages, typed ingredients, and photos of your fridge or pantry) is transmitted to and processed by:

  • Anthropic (provider of Claude) — https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — https://openai.com/privacy

These providers process your inputs in order to generate responses. We only transmit the minimum information needed for each request (the message or image you sent). We do not transmit your name, email address, or account ID to these providers.

Please review Anthropic's and OpenAI's privacy policies if you want to understand how they handle data on their end. Both are well-known AI companies with published data handling practices.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your account data and app content are stored on secure cloud servers hosted by Railway.

We keep your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except for information we must briefly retain for fraud-prevention or legal reasons.

Photos you submit for fridge or pantry analysis are handled transiently: they are passed through Cloudflare R2 and to third-party AI providers only to complete your request, and are not stored permanently by Dinner Drama.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data (see below)
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings

To delete your account and data: You can request account deletion by contacting us at support@duraventi.com. We will process your request within 30 days. Note that some data may be retained for legal or fraud-prevention purposes for a limited period after deletion.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or another jurisdiction with applicable privacy laws, you may have additional rights under those laws (such as GDPR or CCPA). Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

6. Children's Privacy

Dinner Drama is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information — including encrypted passwords, secure connections (HTTPS), and access controls on our systems. No system is 100% secure, but we take this seriously.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you through the app or by email before the changes take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

9. Contact Us

Questions, requests, or concerns about your privacy? We're reachable at:

support@duraventi.com

We aim to respond within 5 business days.
